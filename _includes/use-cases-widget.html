For Prescription Drug Plan Sponsors:

Provide FHIR-formatted bulk claims data about plan enrollees to stand-alone Prescription Drug Plan (PDP) sponsors in accordance with Bipartisan Budget Act of 2018 (BBA). Claims data can help PDP sponsors to better promote the appropriate use of medications and treatment plans in order to improve health outcomes. This data can also reduce a patient's burden in providing their medical history.
